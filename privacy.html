Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

Introduction

Raley Ventures ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard information in connection with our SMS notification service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you opt in to receive SMS notifications from Raley Ventures:

  • Phone number
  • Name (if provided)
  • Message content sent to and from our SMS number

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the purpose of sending and receiving SMS notifications related to Raley Ventures business operations, including:

  • Account notifications and alerts
  • Appointment and meeting reminders
  • Business updates and task notifications

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information may be shared only with our SMS service provider (Twilio) for the purpose of delivering messages.

Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide the SMS notification service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of SMS notifications at any time by replying STOP
  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
