Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

Introduction

Raley Ventures ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard information in connection with our SMS notification service operated under the program name "Raley Ventures SMS Notifications" (the "Jasper AI executive assistant").

Information We Collect

When you opt in to receive SMS notifications from Raley Ventures, we collect:

  • Mobile phone number
  • Name (if provided on the opt-in form)
  • Opt-in timestamp and IP address
  • Message content sent to and from our SMS number

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the purpose of sending and receiving SMS notifications related to Raley Ventures business operations, including:

  • Meeting and appointment reminders
  • Task deadline alerts
  • Calendar change notifications
  • Account activity confirmations

Mobile Information and SMS Data Sharing

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services, such as customer service and SMS delivery providers (Twilio), is permitted solely to deliver messages. We do not sell, rent, distribute, or trade your personal data to third parties without your explicit consent.

All SMS opt-in data — including your mobile number and consent record — is excluded from any sharing, sale, or transfer to third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Service Providers

We use Twilio, Inc. as our SMS delivery provider. Twilio receives your mobile phone number and message content strictly for the purpose of routing and delivering SMS messages you have consented to receive. Twilio is contractually bound to protect this information and may not use it for any other purpose. For details, see Twilio's privacy policy at https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy.

Data Retention

We retain your opt-in record and message history only for as long as necessary to operate the SMS notification service and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at kyle@raleyventures.com.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of SMS notifications at any time by replying STOP to any message
  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

Children's Privacy

Our SMS notification service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
